The Adventure Series

Welcome!

Welcome to the North Coast Adventure Series. What is the North Coast Adventure Series you ask. We are a curated, self-guided cycling series for those seeking adventure along the roads and trails less traveled in and around Humboldt County, California. The Adventure Series allows you to ride the routes at your leisure, take beautiful photos, and win prizes!

Who Can Participate?

Anyone! You must have a Strava account (free account is fine) with your activity logged for submission.

When?

The Series will run through 2021, ending on December 31, 2021. Youâ€™ll have the entire year to complete the routes.

What Are the Routes?

For 2021 we are focusing on loop routes. While not all routes will be complete loops, most will consist of rides that start and end at the same location without much overlap. Because we love all types of cycling, the routes will cover all types of terrain from road rides, gravel roads, and single-track mountain bike trails. To see the selected routes in detail, please visit the Routes page.

How Does the Series Work?

Simple! Ride a route, sign in to the website, submit your ride information, win prizes! Once the ride is verified you see it listed in your dashboard. For full Adventure Series details please visit the guidelines page.
